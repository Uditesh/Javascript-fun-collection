> Player One will go first!
> Two Dices will be thrown at the same time and total of those will be the current score of the player.
> Now here if the number of two dices are same then current score will be zero AND another player will have turn.
> Now, Here is the catch. To not to lose current score, players will have to hold their scores.
> A player wins who reaches 150 score first.
> Enjoy the game but take care as it is addictive. 😉
